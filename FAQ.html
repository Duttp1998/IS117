FAQ

WorkSmarter not harder

Should I workout Under Stress?

Exercise discharges endorphins, which will calm you down. It is additionally best to channel that worry into something beneficial like exercise than to release it on your life partner, children, puppy or self.

How long does it take to see results?

You may be able to see results in the first 6-12 weeks. This depends in large part to your nutrition. You can work out as hard as you would like. If you do not eat right, you will not get far.

Should I eat late at night or before bed?

This shouldn't matter since it depends on the individuals daily caloric intake. If an individual has 500 calories before sleep, it is ok to consume those calories. Since the individual is in a defeciet those additional 500 calories would bring them to maintenance.

I am on a very low carb diet, is that good?

Low carb diet has taken the fitness industry by storm. People have seen great results doing a low carb diet. Yet it is not sustainable. Being a slave to the food you eat is not enjoyable. Missing out on pizza and soda is not enjoyable. Living live to fullest and enjoying food is what will make a diet plan sustainable and successful.
