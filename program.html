Programming for Everyone

WorkSmarter not harder

Easy Diffuculty Workout

Frequency Per Week: 3

This workout if for people who have begun their fitness journey and would like to stay fit

# Excercise Sets Reps
1 Pullups 4 Failure
2 Pushups 5 8-10
3 Squats 5 10-15

Medium Difficulty Workout

Frequency Per Week: 3

This workout if for people who have are in their intermediate stage of their fitness journey and looking to put on a decent amount of muscle and stay fit.

# Excercise Sets Reps
1 Pullups 4 10-15
2 Assisted One-Arm Pushups 5 10-13 each side
3 Assisted Pistol Squats 5 8-10

Advanced Difficulty Workout

Frequency Per Week: 4

This workout if for people who are looking to be strong as well as have a great looking physique and endurance.

*Giant Set- (Easy variation of easy excersices to be done back to back)= 1 Set

Do 3 Giant sets with 2 minute rest in between

# Excercise Sets Reps
1 Weighted Pullups 4 6-8
2 One-Arm Pushups 5 8-10 each side
3 Pistol Squats 5 8-10
5 GiantSet 3 Rounds Failure
